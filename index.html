Shelf Life List

  • Read - USDA KNOWLEDGE ARTICLE
  • Date format - Example:
      MM DDD Y
      MMM DD YY
  • Each soda product will has different shelf life (opened, stored in dry/cool area, refrigerated)
  • Sodas can last 3 to 9 months or longer (unopened)
  • For best quality, consume unopened diet sodas within 3 months after the date expires; regular sodas within 9 months
  • Monster drinks have shelf life of 2yrs.
  • To get expiration date: Add the estimated shelf life with the Manufacture Date -- 2yrs + 2023 = 2025 || Oct + 3mo = Jan

Please read before using

  • **This will not be 100% accurate for some brands, please do reseach & be extra careful when checking for Manufacture Date**
  • Manufacture Date also called Sell-By-Date.
  • If you're in 2023 and the can has number 0 means it was made in 2020, not the year it will expired (2030)
  • If you see 15:54 this means Time (3:54pm).
  • Year list will update every 10 years, if it doesn't. Contact me
